About Twidector

With the increasing popularity of social media over the years, there is also a noticeable increase of hateful content being exchanged or shared amongst users. Therefore, we have decided to create and propose an application, Twidector, that is capable of identifying these hateful content as negative posts and categorising their context such as fake news, fraud or hateful words. Hateful words would then be further categorised as racist, terrorism, etc.

With Twidector, users will be able to search and scan through any Twitter user with their respective profile url. With a click, they are able to identify what sort of user they are and what content they retweet or share among their peers/friends. This is all thanks to our database and machine-learning algorithm which will identify and categorise their tweets. We also give suggestions on potential users that you may recognise that are also known to be negative users through our user database.
